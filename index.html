About IC3

Understanding the Internet Crime Complaint Center

IC3 (Internet Crime Complaint Center) is an official partnership between the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI), the National White Collar Crime Center (NW3C), and the Bureau of Justice Assistance (BJA).

IC3 serves as the nation's central hub for reporting and investigating internet-enabled crime. We work tirelessly to protect citizens from cyber fraud, identity theft, cryptocurrency scams, and financial crimes.

Our Core Services

  • Crime Reporting: Secure and encrypted reporting of internet crimes
  • Asset Recovery: Assistance in recovering lost cryptocurrency and digital assets
  • Investigation Support: Coordination with law enforcement agencies worldwide
  • Victim Assistance: Comprehensive support for fraud victims
  • Prevention Education: Resources to prevent future cyber crimes

Our Mission

Provide a secure platform for reporting internet crime and coordinating recovery efforts to protect citizens and their assets.

Security First

All information is encrypted with military-grade security and protected under federal privacy laws.

IC3 Recovery Process

1

Case Registration

Your case is registered in our secure database and assigned a unique case number for tracking.

2

Initial Investigation

Our team conducts a comprehensive investigation of the fraud or theft claim you've reported.

3

Asset Tracing

We trace your lost assets across blockchain networks and coordinate with exchanges.

4

Recovery Coordination

Once assets are located, we coordinate with authorities for secure recovery and transfer.

5

Final Verification

A small verification fee may be required to finalize the recovery process and complete all formalities.

6

Asset Transfer

Upon verification, your recovered assets are securely transferred to your designated wallet.
